What applications are available for Artificial Intelligence?

Home Problems Goals Applications


AI is relevant to any intellectual task and modern AI techniques are pervasive. When a technique reaches mainstream use, it is no longer onsidered artificial intelligence. This was known as the AI effect. A high profile example of AI use would be autonomous vehicles, medical dianosis, creating art, proving mathematical theorems, playing games, search engines, online assistants, image recognition in photographs, spam filtering, prediction of judicial decisions and targeting online advertisements. As social media has slowly taken over, major publishers started using AI to post stories more effectively and generate higher volumes of traffic.

Artificial intelligence broke into healthcare industry by assisting doctors. According to Bloomberg Technology, Microsoft has developed AI to help doctors find the right treatments for cancer. There is a great amount of research and drugs developed relating to cancer. There are more than 800 medicines and vaccines to treat cancer. This negatively affects the doctors because there are too many options to choose from, making it more difficult to choose the right drugs for patients.

Advancements in AI have contributed to the growth of the automotive industry through the creation and evolution of self-driving vehicles. As of 2016, there are over 30 companies utilizing AI into the creation of driverless cars. Many of the componets that contribute to self-driving cars are braking, lane changing, collision prevention, navigation and mapping. Recent developments in autonomous automobiles have made the innovation of self-driving trucks possible. One of the main factors that influences the ability for a driver-less vehicle is mapping.

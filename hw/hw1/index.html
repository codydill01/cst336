Artificial Intelligence is intelligence demonstrated by machines. AI research is defined as the study of "intelligent agents": any device that perceives its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of successfully achieving its goals. It was founded as an academic discipline in 1956, and in the years since has experienced serveral waves of optimism.

Artificial intelligence has progressed rapidly throughout the years. Having AI on mobile devices to having the abilitiy to have your car drive itself, it seems as if the possibilities are endless. AI can encompass anything from Google's search algorithms to IBM's Watson to autonomous weapons. The idea of AI is to simple increase efficiency throughout the day to allow businesses to be more effective and allow people more time.

The push that is presented to attach all activities with an AI assistance brings a question to mind; can AI be dangerous? Switching gears to autonomous weapons they are dangerous because if they come into the hands of the wrong person it can cause mass casualities. Moreover, an AI arms race could inadvertently lead to an AI war that also results in mass casualities.

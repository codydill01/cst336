The research goal of AI is to create technology that allows computers and machines to work together in a intelligent manner. The problem of simulating intelligence has had many sub issues along the way during the early phases. These consisted of particular traits that researchers expect an intelligent system to display.

During the early phases of AI algorithems were developed that imitagted step-by-step reasoning that humans used when solving puzzles. They also created algorithems to understand the human logical deductions. In the late 1980s and 1990s, AI research would develop methods for dealing with uncertain or incomplete information and empoly concepts from probability and economics.

Knowledge representation are central to classical AI research. A few of the expert systems attempted to gather together explicit knowledge possessed by experts in some narrow doamin. Thus, commonsense knowledge was gathered and stored in a database to have a general understanding of the world. The database contained objects, properties, causes and effects, and many other less researched domains.
